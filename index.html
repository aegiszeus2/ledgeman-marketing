Everything You Need

Built for the way you actually work

No bloat. No $500/month price tags. Just the tools a PM needs to run a tight job site.

🏗️
Project Management

Track every active job — budget vs. actual, subtasks, progress, and assigned workers. Full project detail view with cost breakdown.

✅
Time Approval Workflow

Workers submit hours and photos. You review and approve or reject from your admin panel. Every entry logged, nothing slips through.

📄
Professional Invoicing

Generate HST-compliant invoices in seconds. Track payment status, holdbacks, and send directly from the app.

👷
Worker Invite & 2FA

Invite workers via secure one-time link. They set their own PIN and optionally enable two-factor authentication with Google Authenticator.

⏱
Clock In / Clock Out

Workers tap Clock In when they start, Clock Out when they're done. Hours auto-calculate and round to the nearest 15 minutes.

📸
Job Site Photo Log

Workers attach photos to every time entry. Photos are grouped by date and worker — exportable as PDF or CSV for client reports.

💰
Expense Tracking

Log material costs, vendor invoices, and labour expenses by project. See real profit margins, not guesses.

📈
Reports & Analytics

Financial summaries, labour breakdowns, invoice aging, and cost-per-project reports — all exportable.

🔒
Secure & Private

Your data stays in your browser. No third-party servers storing your job data. Full backup and restore at any time.
